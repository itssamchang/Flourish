Monstera adansonii

Pests

Monsteras can be susceptible to mealybugs, spider mites, gnats and scale. Check your plants regularly and quarantine any new plants you bring home.

Treatments range from rinsing with water to applying neem oil, isopropyl alcohol or food-grade diatomaceous earth to leaves. Repotting, removing the top layer of soil and beneficial nematodes may also help control infestations. Sometimes fertilizer can discourage pests as well.

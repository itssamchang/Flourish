Monstera Adansonii Care Card

LIGHT

Provide bright, indirect light. It can tolerate low light, but will not grow as fast or as large. Avoid direct sunlight as it can burn your monstera’s leaves.

WATER

Water when the top two inches of soil is dry. Underwatering is preferred to overwatering to avoid root rot. Water less frequently in the winter.

SOIL

Plant in a peat-rich, well-draining soil mix. Coco fiber is a sustainable alternative to peat moss. Repot every 1-3 years.

HUMIDITY

Higher humidity promotes growth. Gently mist once a week depending on the climate where you live.

SUPPORT

This is plant grows like a vine. Providing a moss pole, stake, or trellis for it to climb promotes larger growth.

FERTILIZE

Fertilize in the spring or summer. Let it rest in the winter and make sure not to over fertilize as the salt in it can burn your plant’s roots.

Monstera adansonii

Humidity & temperature

As tropical plants, Monstera adansonii thrive in high humidity ranging anywhere from 60-90%. Low humidity will cause your plant’s leaves to wilt and their tips to brown. They will also wilt if the temperature ranges outside the 64°F to 81°F range.

Keep your Monstera inside and keep humidity levels up for a happy and healthy plant!

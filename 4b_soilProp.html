Monstera adansonii

Repotting

Monstera adansonii is a tropical plant that grows on the rainforest floor and prefers a peat-rich and well-draining soil mix. Coco fiber is a sustainable alternative to peat moss.

Repotting is best in the spring or summer every year or two but can also be done in early fall if you get mild winters. Choose a potting mix.

½ potting soil, ½ coco fiber

½ potting soil, ½ orchid bark or coco chips

¾ potting soil, ¼ perlite or pumice

½ potting soil, ½ coco fiber or peat moss
